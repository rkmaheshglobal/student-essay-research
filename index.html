🏆 Strategy

What Winning Essays Have in Common

Based on analysis of past JLI and Cambridge winning essays.

01
Original thesis that goes beyond the obvious — challenge the framing of the question
02
Engages seriously with the strongest counterarguments, not strawmen
03
Uses specific data and named academic sources — no vague “studies show”
04
Applies economic theory precisely — Pareto, Kaldor-Hicks, consumer surplus
05
Clear, memorable conclusion that synthesises rather than merely summarises
