What Wins

Five principles that separate winning essays from the rest.

01
Clear, specific, arguable thesis stated in the introduction
02
Academic sources from professor reading lists — not Wikipedia
03
Full paragraph dedicated to the strongest counterargument
04
Distinction between types or cases — not all X is the same
05
Conclusion with a broader implication, not just a summary
