## Essay Writing Principles

**What wins:**
1. Clear, specific, arguable thesis stated in the introduction
2. Academic sources from professor reading lists (not Wikipedia)
3. Full paragraph dedicated to the strongest counterargument
4. Distinction between types or cases (not all X is the same)
5. Conclusion with a broader implication, not just a summary

**What loses:**
- Summarising without taking a position
- Vague thesis ("this is a complex issue")
- Any AI writing assistance (both competitions use AI checkers)
- Ignoring the counterargument
- Exceeding the word limit
